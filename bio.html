About Taihg:

Taihg likes to create things from sleek, snazzy websites to even his own music (find it attached at the bottom of the page).

He first started creating images on 'Photoshop' at the age of 11 on good old cs2... and 10 years later he still hasn't upgraded!

His love of all things graphical catapulted him into the world of graphic design, he started his own mini business online selling YouTube backgrounds and display pictures.

He then went on to start selling logo designs to professional clients at the age of 16, this only wetted his appetite to gain a thirst of knowledge on more than just 'Photoshop'.

At college he gained basic foundations and understanding of web building, in classes such as web development and client side programming.

In his 2 year period at college he went on to win an in-house website competition, leading to his group getting invited to Google HQ London to go head to head with other colleges.

Unfortunately he and his group came runners up in an intense interview process, this did not knock his confidence as he has now moved onto a new challenge, he has come to the University of Northampton to study Business Computing (Web Design) and who knows what he will do next!
